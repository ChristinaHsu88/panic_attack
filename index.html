Don't Panic!

Spend the day as Penny-Ann, your average, everyday woman who struggles with anxiety. Help her navigate her world in a way that doesn't send her stress levels through the roof. As you steer Penny-Ann through her house you'll be presented with a series of options. To find out what those options are, walk up to an item and hit enter. Use the arrow keys to make a decision, hit enter again, and watch the impact it has on Penny-Ann's stess bar. At the end of each game, you will see a chart pop up at the bottom of the screen. The goal of this game is to keep Penny-Ann calm, and keep that chart as balanced as possible.
